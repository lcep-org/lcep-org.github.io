Outreach

In the years since this program was established (a decade ago already!), we’ve undergone some serious changes. What began as a girls-helping-girls initiative to empower through enrichment and self-defense at Thousand Waves Martial Arts & Self-Defense Center in Chicago, IL evolved into first a music program and later a fully established STEM curriculum.
